# Bright-Coffee-Shop-Analysis
Conducting business insights to support the CEO in improving revenue performance and optimizing product strategy.

# Project Purpose

Analyze historical sales data from Bright Coffee Shop to help improve revenue and optimize product strategy for better business performance.

# Tools Used
SQL, Excel/CSV, and data visualization tools were used for data extraction, analysis, and presentation.

# Key Focus Areas

Product performance
Customer behavior
Sales trends across time

# Main Insights

A few top products generate most of the revenue
Sales peak during specific times of the day
Customer buying patterns vary by time and product type
Some product categories show steady growth

# Recommendations

Promote high-performing products
Schedule staff based on peak hours
Run marketing campaigns during slow periods
Adjust inventory according to demand trends

# Conclusion

The project demonstrates how data analytics can uncover valuable insights and support strategic decisions to drive business growth.
